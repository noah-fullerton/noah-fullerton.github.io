About the Project

Created a complete gameplay framework that extends the UE5 variant shooter template, including enemy AI, rebuildable barricades, purchaseable wall weapons and ammo, a mystery box, powerup drops, and melee and grenade mechanics.

Key Features

Zombie Enemy AI

I used UE5 state trees to implement Zombie AI. Each enemy spawnpoint has a set barricade, so zombies spawn and while they are outside of the level, their main task is to approach, break, and enter their assigned barricade. After entering, they are now inside the level so they can chase the player. No matter success or fail, they will always chase player until they are close enough to attack, then attack, then repeat.

Barricade System

Each barricade has invisible planes on the inside and outside of level, so zombies move to the outside plane, when the barricade is broken they can move to the inside pane. Barricades can be rebuilt by player, and award points for doing so. Planes are visible here just so you can see how it works.

Mystery Box

Mystery Box is a child class of my purchaseable class, if player can interact and has enough money to purchase box, it will subtract money and choose a weapon. It checks player owned weapons, and removes them from the available weapon array, to avoid duplicates. After it chooses a weapon, opens the lid, and spawns it, you have the opportunity to secondary interact, swapping your current weapon with the box weapon. Alternatively, if you don't want this gun, you can just wait and it will close itself and reset.
